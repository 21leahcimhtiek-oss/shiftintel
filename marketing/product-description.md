# ShiftIntel — Product Description

## One-Line
AI-driven workforce scheduling that eliminates coverage gaps and cuts overtime costs by 25%.

## Short (50 words)
ShiftIntel uses GPT-4o to build optimized weekly schedules in 30 seconds. Coverage gaps are detected in real-time, labor costs are forecasted 4 weeks ahead, and compliance rules are enforced automatically. Built for retail, healthcare, hospitality, and any shift-based operation.

## Long (200 words)
ShiftIntel is an enterprise SaaS platform that transforms how businesses manage their workforce schedules. Instead of spending 4-8 hours per week manually building shift schedules in spreadsheets, operations managers use ShiftIntel's AI engine — powered by GPT-4o — to generate optimized schedules in under 30 seconds.

The AI considers every employee's availability, time-off requests, skill requirements, and maximum hours to create shifts that meet coverage requirements while minimizing overtime costs. A real-time coverage score (0-100%) tells managers at a glance whether their schedule is sufficient.

Beyond scheduling, ShiftIntel forecasts labor costs for the next 4 weeks with confidence intervals, giving finance and operations teams visibility into labor budget before the week starts. The compliance engine automatically flags overtime violations, break requirement issues, and max-hours breaches.

ShiftIntel is built for teams from 10 to 10,000 employees, with plans starting at $59/month. The platform integrates with existing payroll systems and provides a REST API for enterprise customers building custom integrations.

**Key differentiators vs. legacy tools (Kronos, ADP):**
- GPT-4o reasoning (not just rule-based)
- Self-serve onboarding (no implementation fee)
- Modern UX that employees actually use
- Real-time coverage intelligence
- Predictive labor cost forecasting
